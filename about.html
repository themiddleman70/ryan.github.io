Background

Although being born in Iowa in 1995, I am currently living in Texas. However, this wasn't my initial move. After leaving Iowa, I moved to Georgia, Utah, and Texas, where I graduated high school at Friendswood High School in 2014 as well as receiving an Associate of Science in Computer Science in December 2016 at San Jacinto College, along with a Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics and a Minor in Computer Science in May 2019 from Texas State University.

After receiving my bachelor's degree, I had made it my goal to move back to Utah because I felt that Utah was home to me due to the beautiful views that the state has to offer. Not only that, but I had made some very good friends while living there that I'm still in contact to this day. In November 2020, that dream happened as I received a job offer from SelectHealth in Salt Lake City. I lived and worked there for the next 2.5 years. However, in July 2023, some personal issues arose to where I could no longer live and work in Utah, so I moved back to Texas to live with my parents, where I am still to this day. However, my dream is to move back up there to Utah, and hopefully continue to live and work there for as long as I can.
